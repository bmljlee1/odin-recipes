Lasagne Recipe

Description

This classic lasagne recipe is the ultimate comfort food. Layers of rich, meaty sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta come together for a hearty and satisfying dish that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 250g lasagne sheets
  • 100g grated cheese
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the lasagne sheets, meat sauce, and béchamel sauce.
  6. Top with grated cheese.
  7. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
