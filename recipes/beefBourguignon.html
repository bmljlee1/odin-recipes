Beef Bourguignon Recipe

Description

Beef Bourguignon is a traditional French stew made with tender beef braised in red wine and flavored with rich herbs, garlic, and onions. It’s a comforting dish that’s perfect for a special dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg stewing beef, cubed
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 200g mushrooms, quartered
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. In a large pot, brown the beef on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté the onion, garlic, and carrots until soft.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add the beef back to the pot along with the wine, beef stock, and bay leaf.
  6. Bring to a boil, then cover and transfer to the oven to braise for 2 to 3 hours.
  7. In the last 30 minutes, add the mushrooms and continue cooking.
  8. Remove from the oven and season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
